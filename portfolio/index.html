About Me

Hey there. I'm Wynne Yao, a computer science and sound design student at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I'm interested in finding innovative and sophisticated ways to use technology in creating positive change in the world. So far, my interests lay in web development. I also love fusing art and technology to create impactful projects.

I like to spend my time creating playlists, playing piano, and failing to get people to laugh at my self-deprecating jokes.
